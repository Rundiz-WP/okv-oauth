Use this command:
`wp i18n make-pot . --exclude="assets/vendor,blocks-src,languages,node_modules,node_tasks,*.css,*.svg,*.eot,*.woff,*.woff2,*.ttf,*.html,*.txt,*.pot"`
And check the result.

Convert to JSON to make block i18n supported with this command:
`wp i18n make-json languages/okv-oauth-th.po --no-purge --pretty-print`.
